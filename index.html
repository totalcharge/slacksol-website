Nano

  • Sub-50ms response times at the edge, globally
  • Automatic image, script, and markup optimization
  • Real-user monitoring with adaptive routing

Dynamic Site Accelerator

Dynamic content can't be cached - but it can still be fast. DSA uses proprietary route optimization and persistent origin connections to accelerate every page, no matter how personalized.

Fast DNS

Your site is only as fast as its DNS. Fast DNS answers queries from the edge node closest to each user, cutting resolution time and keeping your domain reachable even under attack.

  • Answers DNS queries in under 20ms globally

  • Absorbs multi-terabit DDoS against DNS infrastructure

  • Health-driven failover in under 30 seconds

  • Works across AWS, Azure, GCP, and on-prem

IP Application Accelerator

  • Transparent failover - no client-side changes required

Media Delivery

Buffer spinners kill audiences. Ours don't.

Deliver broadcast-quality experiences at internet scale — with the analytics to prove it.

Adaptive Media Delivery

AMD monitors each viewer's network conditions in real time and seamlessly shifts bitrate so playback stays smooth - even when connectivity degrades mid-stream.

  • Reduces rebuffering events by up to 40%
  • Supports HLS, DASH, and CMAF at any scale
  • Built-in origin shield to reduce egress costs

Download Delivery

A stalled download is a lost customer. Multipart transfer, intelligent retry logic, and edge proximity push completion rates for large files - software, game clients, firmware - to near 100%.

  • Resume-capable transfers survive connection drops
  • Real-time download funnel analytics
  • Token-based access control and rate limiting

Infinite Media Acceleration

For broadcast-quality streaming at internet scale. IMA provides dedicated ingest infrastructure, redundant paths, and ultra-low-latency last-mile delivery for live events of any size.

  • Sub-second glass-to-glass latency options
  • Redundant ingest with automatic failover
  • Scales to millions of concurrent viewers in minutes

Dynamic Site Delivery

Serve full-page experiences - headers, footers, personalization blocks and all - from the edge. DSD dramatically cuts origin load while keeping every response fresh and user-specific.

  • Offload up to 85% of page requests from origin
  • Granular cache control at the component level
  • Works with any CMS or e-commerce platform

NetStorage

A globally replicated object store that feeds directly into the delivery network. No egress fees between storage and CDN, no latency tax, no single region becoming a bottleneck.

  • Synchronous replication across three geographic zones
  • Direct ingest from production workflows
  • Fine-grained access control via API or UI

Media Services

From raw ingest to viewer-ready stream in one pipeline. Media Services handles transcoding, DRM licensing, and adaptive packaging so you focus on content, not plumbing.

  • Encode once, deliver everywhere (HLS, DASH, MP4)
  • Multi-DRM: Widevine, FairPlay, PlayReady
  • Frame-accurate clipping and just-in-time packaging

Media Analytics

You can't improve what you don't measure. A real-time window into QoE metrics, audience behavior, and CDN performance at both the viewer level and the segment level.

  • Tracks buffering ratio, startup time, and bitrate
  • Audience segmentation by geography, device, and ISP
  • Webhooks and API access for BI tool integration
Cloud Security

Your perimeter is the internet. Defend it.

Enterprise-grade protection that stops attacks at the edge — before they ever reach your infrastructure.

Cloud Site Defender

Combines a high-capacity DDoS scrubbing network with an adaptive WAF to block volumetric attacks, credential stuffing, and zero-day exploits before they reach your origin.

  • 20+ Tbps of DDoS mitigation capacity
  • OWASP Top 10 coverage with auto-updated rule sets
  • PCI DSS and SOC 2 compliant by design

Bot Manager

Uses behavioral fingerprinting and ML to distinguish humans, good bots, and malicious automation - protecting your infrastructure, analytics, and pricing from abuse.

  • Detects headless browsers, proxies, and CAPTCHA solvers
  • Challenge, block, or feed misinformation to bad bots
  • Protects checkout flows, login pages, and APIs

Cloud DDoS Defender

When a multi-vector attack hits, mitigation needs to start in seconds - not minutes. Our always-on scrubbing architecture absorbs attack traffic at the network edge before it enters your infrastructure.

  • Sub-60-second time-to-mitigate for volumetric attacks
  • Covers UDP, TCP, and ICMP flood vectors simultaneously
  • Dedicated 24/7 incident response team

DNS Defender

DNS is the first step in every web transaction - and one of the most attacked protocols online. Anycast routing and inline DDoS absorption keep your domain online when others go dark.

  • DNSSEC support for all zones
  • NXDOMAIN filtering and DNS firewall
  • Real-time query analytics for threat hunting

Client Reputation

Why respond to attackers you already know? Client Reputation taps into global visibility across trillions of daily requests to pre-emptively block IPs with a history of malicious behavior.

  • Reputation scores updated in real time, globally
  • Configurable response: block, rate-limit, or challenge
  • Reduces WAF false positives by pre-filtering bad actors

Site Shield

Attackers can bypass your CDN if they find your origin IP. Site Shield keeps it secret by routing all inbound traffic through our scrubbing infrastructure - your servers become unreachable to direct attack.

  • Hides your origin from DNS enumeration and IP scanning
  • Works alongside existing firewall and WAF rules
  • Audit-ready access logs for every inbound request
